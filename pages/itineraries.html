Hotel check-in, city orientation and welcome dinner. Transfer to Kithulgala.

2
Day 3 · Kithulgala Rafting

White water rafting on Kelani River, cliff jumping and waterfall hike.

3
Day 4–5 · Horton Plains & Adam's Peak

Sunrise hike to Adam's Peak (5,200 steps) and World's End cliff at Horton Plains.

4
Day 6 · Ella Rock Hike

Train to Ella, trek Ella Rock and Little Adam's Peak, Nine Arch Bridge sunset.

5
Day 7–8 · Yala Safari

Two game drives in Yala — leopards, sloth bears, elephants, crocodiles.

6
Day 9 · Mirissa Whale Watching

Early morning boat for blue whales & dolphins in the Indian Ocean.

7
Day 10 · Surfing at Hikkaduwa

Surf lesson or reef snorkelling at one of Sri Lanka's best surf spots.

Wildlife Tours

Sri Lanka is one of the world's great wildlife destinations — five national parks, whale watching and endemic birds.

8–12 Days Expert Naturalist Nov–May Best
1
Days 1–2 · Wilpattu National Park

Sri Lanka's largest park — leopards in villa (wewa) grassland habitats.

2
Days 3–4 · Minneriya / Kaudulla

The famous "Elephant Gathering" — 300+ elephants in one location July–October.

3
Days 5–6 · Yala Block 1

Highest leopard concentration on Earth. Dawn and dusk jeep drives.

4
Day 7 · Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home

Watch orphaned elephant calves fed by keepers, then evening game drive.

5
Day 8 · Mirissa Whale Watching

Blue whale sunrise encounter and spinner dolphin pods.
