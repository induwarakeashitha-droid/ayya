Explore the 5th-Century Sky Palace

Rising 200 metres above the central plains, Sigiriya Rock Fortress is one of the most dramatic archaeological sites in the world. Built by King Kashyapa in the 5th century AD, this UNESCO World Heritage Site features ancient frescoes, the famous Mirror Wall, and the monumental Lion Gate — all perched atop a volcanic plug surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens.

Our private Sigiriya tours pair you with an SLTDA-licensed guide who brings the fortress's remarkable history to life — from the royal water gardens to the cloud maidens frescoes.

Sample 3-Day Itinerary

Day1

Arrival → Sigiriya Region

Airport pickup and scenic drive to Sigiriya. Afternoon visit to Dambulla Royal Cave Temple — five cave shrines adorned with 153 Buddha statues and 2,100 sq m of murals.

Day2

Sigiriya Rock Fortress & Village Safari

Early morning climb of Sigiriya Rock Fortress with your expert guide. Afternoon village experience — oxcart ride, catamaran on the lake, and a home-cooked Sri Lankan lunch.

Day3

Pidurangala Sunrise & Minneriya Safari

Pre-dawn trek up Pidurangala Rock for a stunning sunrise view of Sigiriya. Afternoon jeep safari at Minneriya National Park. Transfer onward or to airport.
