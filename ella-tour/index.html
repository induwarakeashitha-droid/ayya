The Most Scenic Corner of Sri Lanka

Ella is a tiny highland town that has stolen the hearts of travellers worldwide. Perched at 1,041 metres in Sri Lanka's hill country, it offers jaw-dropping views of tea-draped valleys, cascading waterfalls, and the most Instagram-famous railway bridge on the planet — the Nine Arch Bridge.

The journey to Ella is as spectacular as the destination itself. The Kandy-to-Ella train ride is consistently ranked as one of the most scenic railway journeys in the world, winding through emerald tea estates, colonial-era tunnels, and cloud-kissed mountain passes.

Our private Ella tours combine the best hiking, sightseeing, and local experiences — all at your own pace with a dedicated guide and driver.

Sample 2-Day Itinerary

Day1

Scenic Train to Ella & Nine Arch Bridge

Board the iconic blue train in Kandy or Nuwara Eliya for the world-famous scenic journey to Ella. Arrive in the afternoon, check in to your boutique hotel, and walk to the Nine Arch Bridge to watch the train cross at golden hour. Evening at leisure in Ella town.

Day2

Little Adam's Peak & Ravana Falls

Early morning hike to Little Adam's Peak summit for 360° panoramic views of Ella Gap and the southern plains. Visit a working tea factory for a guided tour and tasting. Afternoon stop at Ravana Falls — one of the island's widest waterfalls at 25 metres. Optional zipline adventure over the Ella valley.
